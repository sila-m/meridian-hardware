Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Stocked for Every Job

Meridian Hardware is Nairobi's go-to hardware destination, conveniently located at Kangema House on Munyu Road. We serve contractors, builders, homeowners, and DIY enthusiasts with an extensive range of quality products — from hand tools and power equipment to plumbing fittings, electrical supplies, and heavy construction materials.

Our experienced team is committed to helping you find exactly what you need, quickly and affordably. Whether you're fixing a leaky pipe, wiring a new room, or completing a major construction project, Meridian Hardware has you covered with honest advice and the right stock.
